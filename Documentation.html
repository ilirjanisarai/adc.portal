• Mandatory Documents for New Activations (Mobile & Fixed Internet)  TLM CHANNEL

 

Personal data are required for registration prior to service activation according to Law no. 9918, date 19.05.2008, on Electronic Communications in the Republic of Albania. Hence, it is important to collect the correct customer information and in compliance with company policies and procedures.

The customer registration documents collected per customer type, hereafter are part of Customer Registration Documents (CRD)

 

 

 

Fixed Internet Residential Customer 

 

  1. Valid Passport or ID Card

  2. Physical address 

  3. Contact No.

  4. E-Mail address

 

Fixed Internet Business Customer

 

 

  1. Valid Passport or ID Card

  2. Physical address

  3. Contact No. 

  4. Business Register Extract (Tax ID No included)

  5. E-Mail address

 

Mobile Business Customer- New Connection (Prepaid to Postpaid & Prepaid to Hybrid)

 

  1. Administrator’s valid Passport or ID Card

  2. Business Register Extract (Tax ID No included)

  3. Contact No.

  4. Physical Address

  5. E-Mail address

     

     

     

Mobile Business Customer-New Connection (Portability Activations)

 

  1. Administrator’s valid Passport or ID Card

  2. User’s valid Passport or ID Card under the current Operator

  3. Business Register Extract (Tax ID No included)

  4. Contact No.

  5. Physical Address

  6. Port in official request (Business Stamp and Customer’s signature included)
